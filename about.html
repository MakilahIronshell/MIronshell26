About Me

Hello, I’m Makilah, the photographer behind Makilah Porter Photography. I am based in Broken Bow, Nebraska, and I love capturing genuine moments that families, couples, and individuals can look back on for years to come.

Photography is special to me because it allows me to tell stories through images. My goal is to create a relaxed and welcoming experience so your photos feel natural, joyful, and true to who you are. I want every session to capture real emotions, meaningful connections, and beautiful memories.

I enjoy photographing families, couples, seniors, weddings, and milestone moments. Whether I am documenting a big life event or a simple everyday memory, I believe every season of life deserves to be remembered.
